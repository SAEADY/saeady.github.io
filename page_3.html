Style Guide

For the style, I looked up tech websites and was inspired by their more minimalist futuristic look. I also looked up data and computer terms to find images with colors I liked.

For the interaction and layout I was inspired particularly by the Violence Against Women website which has an easy to use and nice way of going around the website.

For the content I was inspired by the yourdatamirror website, which initially inspired me on the topic and I watched a documentary called the Social Dilemma for more info as well as went searched for research articles and statistics on the issues which is back at the home page
