The Social Business
An Activist Web Intervention on the Business practices of Social Media

Since it’s boom in the early 2000s, social media usage has become a regular occurence in a large majority of people’s lives. 5.04 billion people worldwide use social media, making it used by 62.3% of the population. Average time spent per day is 2h 23 m

Social media allows for both positive and negative impacts on society. On on side, it promotes connectivity, activism, and knowledge sharing. However, it also has risks such as misinformation, mental health, and privacy breaches. While users bear responsibility for their actions, the business practices of social media companies play a crucial role. Improved transparency, ethical data handling, and proactive moderation can mitigate harm.

This website aims to give insight on the operational practices of social media companies and the impact they have on individuals and society at large. Through info and statistics gained from researched articles and insightful documentaries, this website will delve into the multiple ways in which this technology can be harnessed for both positive and negative purposes. By exploring the nuances of social media's influence, this website aims to foster awareness and understanding of social media’s effects on our lives, relationships, and broader society
